# ADR 0001 — Silhouette-first discovery

## Status

Accepted.

## Context

Earlier prototypes focused too quickly on 3D, sliders and parametric control. This created friction and did not match the current design phase.

The project needs to discover the living direction of the form before investing in detailed CAD.

## Decision

The first production-quality app will be Shape Hunt: a silhouette-first browser for hundreds of generated forms.

The first screen prioritizes:

- many black profiles
- fast visual judgement
- save/reject/more-like-this
- shortlist and A/B comparison

## Consequences

Positive:

- faster taste discovery
- less interface noise
- better alignment with early design process
- creates a dataset of preferences

Negative:

- not final CAD
- handles and production details are postponed
- geometry is approximate at this stage
