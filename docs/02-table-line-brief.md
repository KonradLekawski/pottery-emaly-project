# Table Line Brief

## Objective

Design the core family of premium enamel table cups:

- 120 ml
- 240 ml
- 330 ml
- 440 ml

The line should fit a classic table and an aspirational modern home. It should feel premium without becoming cold, and child-friendly without becoming childish.

## Starting proportions

These are not final manufacturing dimensions. They are starting anchors for exploration.

| Size | Height | Base diameter | Rim diameter | Character |
|---:|---:|---:|---:|---|
| 120 ml | 52 mm | 62 mm | 80 mm | low, stable, dignified |
| 240 ml | 68 mm | 72 mm | 94 mm | child, but serious |
| 330 ml | 82 mm | 76 mm | 102 mm | brand DNA |
| 440 ml | 96 mm | 84 mm | 112 mm | generous, not bucket-like |

## Shape vocabulary

### Foot

- table shadow
- subtle undercut
- premium base
- not heavy pedestal

### Body

- soft belly
- controlled warmth
- no cylinder
- no cheap bowl

### Waist

- small tension
- gives soul
- must not become a goblet

### Rim

- soft tulip-like opening
- premium contact point
- not theatrical

### Handle

- wing-like, especially for children
- elegant negative space
- lightweight visual mass

## Key rejection signals

- camping silhouette
- office mug cylinder
- toy-like child cup
- too trendy / fashion object
- too heavy visually
- 440 ml looks like a bucket
- 120 ml looks like a baby product instead of tableware
